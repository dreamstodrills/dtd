Our Services

At Dreams To Drills, we provide personalized design solutions that fit your lifestyle, space, and budget.

Full-Service Design

We manage your entire project—from concept development and space planning to sourcing, purchasing, and final installation—so you can enjoy the transformation stress-free.

E-Design

Get professional design help no matter where you are. Our virtual service includes moodboards, layouts, and a detailed shopping list tailored to your needs and budget.

Renovation Support

You don’t need to have all the answers to get started with us.

Just exploring ideas? Need a solid plan you can run with?

Let’s chat in a Consultation Call.

À La Carte

Pick and choose the services that suit your needs — no packages, just flexibility.

Want beautiful new kitchen cabinets but don’t know where to begin? We’re here to help! Whether you need custom measurements or already have a plan in mind, we’ll design a space you’ll love coming home to.

Need guidance choosing furniture for your space? We’ll work within your budget to curate pieces that suit your style and needs. Whether you’d like us to place the orders or prefer to receive links and shop at your convenience — we’ve got you covered.

Great decor brings warmth, character, and harmony to your design. Whether you want bold statement pieces or subtle finishing touches, we’ll guide you each step of the way. Ready to get started? Let us know if you’d like us to manage the decor orders or send you a personalized shopping list!

We’ll help you choose paint and palette combinations that complement your style, lighting, and furnishings—whether you’re seeking bold statements or timeless neutrals. Enjoy personalized recommendations and convenient options.

We’ll help you select fixtures that seamlessly fit your style and budget, ensuring every space feels inviting and beautifully illuminated. Whether you want statement pieces or subtle accents, we’ll guide you to your perfect lighting solutions.

Choosing the right size and style for custom curtains and blinds can feel overwhelming. We’ll visit your home to measure your windows precisely and help you select the perfect treatments that enhance your space beautifully and effortlessly.

Feeling overwhelmed by appliance choices? We understand! Whether you need appliances that fit your current setup or new ones tailored for a remodeled space, we’re here to help you find the perfect match for your home’s style and needs.

Our first step before any project is an in-home consultation, giving us the chance to understand your personality, style, vision, and wish list for the space. For certain projects, virtual consultations are also available. During the consultation, if measurements are needed, we’ll take those at the same time for your convenience.
